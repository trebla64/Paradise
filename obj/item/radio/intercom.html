Proc Details

proc
local_powernet_check
(/datum/source)

Proc called whenever the intercom's local powernet loses or gains power. Responsible for setting the on variable and calling update_icon().

Normally called after the intercom's local powernet sends the COMSIG_POWERNET_POWER_CHANGE signal, but it can also be called directly. Arguments:

source - the area that just had a power change.
